Online Retail Data Quality & Anomaly Detection Pipeline

1. LIBRARIES: pandas, numpy
2. LOAD DATA: OnlineRetail.csv (latin1 encoding)
3. QUALITY CHECKS:
   - Missing CustomerID: [count]
   - Duplicate Invoices: [count]  
   - Invalid Quantity (<=0): [count]
   - Invalid UnitPrice (<=0): [count]
   - Future Dates: [count]

4. DQ SUMMARY: Failure % + Weighted Score (weights: [0.3,0.3,0.2,0.1,0.1])
   - data_quality_score.csv
   - python_data_quality_summary.csv

5. REVENUE ANOMALY DETECTION:
   - Daily Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice
   - Thresholds: ±2σ from mean
   - Flags: "High Spike" | "Sharp Drop" | "Normal"
   - revenue_anomaly_alert_log.csv

Key Outputs: DQ Score, Summary Tables, Anomaly Alerts
Perfect for production monitoring & portfolio showcase.
